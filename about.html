ABOUT US

This is Shekinah Glory, an African restaurant with assorted food options. We are located on Belair Road in Baltimore. We aim to provide you with the best food catering services. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat, it's a destination for a unique dining experience. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere where everyone can come together to enjoy delicious food and learn more about the rich cultural heritage of Africa. Our knowledgeable staff is always happy to answer any questions you may have about the dishes and ingredients we serve.

OUR HISTORY

Welcome to Shekinah Glory Restaurant, an authentic African restaurant serving delicious and traditional dishes for more than a Decade. Since 2012, we are proud to offer a wide range of flavors and ingredients from different regions of Africa that will transport your taste buds on a culinary journey through the continent. Our menu features a variety of dishes that highlight the diversity of African cuisine, from traditional stews and curries to grilled meats and fresh seafood. We use only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients sourced locally and imported directly from Africa.

OUR MISSION

To become the premier destination for authentic African cuisine in the city, known for our high-quality food, exceptional service, and commitment to cultural education and community engagement. We strive to create a space where people can come together to enjoy delicious food, share stories, and learn more about the rich culinary heritage of Africa.
